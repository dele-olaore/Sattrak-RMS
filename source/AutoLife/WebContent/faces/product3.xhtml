Get moving with your parking management

One of the serious challenges of Urban and Suburban mobility for drivers and road users in accessibility and guidance to parking spaces. Particularly in environments like ours with less developed parking infrastructure and technology, many drivers and road users desire real-time and static on-street and off-street parking data to guide them to places to park.

Sattrak Telematics is cooperating with public and private car park operators in aggregating the most comprehensive parking database that can be accessed by Urban and Suburban drivers and road users via GPS and in-car navigation systems, SattrakAutoLife.ng, online widgets, and Android, iOS and Windows Phone apps.

Sattrak AutoLife Parking Management system will be available soon for Business and Commercial Districts, Shopping Malls, Hotels, Airports, Eateries, Schools, Stadiums, Neighborhoods, Venues and many more locations
